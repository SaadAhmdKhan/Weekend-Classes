FitLife:

Rest and recovery are essential components of any fitness program. Giving the body time to rest and repair between workouts helps prevent overtraining, reduce the risk of injury, and optimize performance. Incorporating rest days into your routine, getting adequate sleep, and practicing relaxation techniques can support overall well-being.

Exercise has been shown to have positive effects on mental health, including reducing symptoms of depression, anxiety, and stress. It can also improve mood, boost self-esteem, and promote better sleep.
