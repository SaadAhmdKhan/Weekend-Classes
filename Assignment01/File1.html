NASA:

NASA,the National Aeronautics and Space Administrationis the United States government agency responsible for the nation's civilian space program and for aeronautics and aerospace research. Established in 1958 NASA has been at the forefront of space exploration, sending astronauts to the moon, launching robotic missions to explore distant planets, and conducting groundbreaking research to advance our understanding of the universe and our place within it. With a mission to "reach for new heights and reveal the unknown for the benefit of humankind," NASA continues to inspire future generations and push the boundaries of what is possible in space exploration.
