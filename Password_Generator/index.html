What makes a password strong?


“Long”

The longer a password, the more secure it is.
A strong password should be at least 10 characters long..

“Complex”

Strong passwords use a combination of
letters, numbers, cases, and symbols to
form an unpredictable string of
characters that doesn't resemble words
or names.

“Unique”

A strong password should be unique to
each account to reduce vulnerability in
the event of a hack..
